<objective>
Restructure the App Router so that legacy OopsBudgeter routes live in the `(legacy)/` route group with their full chrome (PasscodeWrapper, AppProvider, BudgetProvider, PageLayout, Logo, Settings, Achievements, ThemeToggle, GoToTop) intact, while the root layout shrinks to truly-global concerns only (html, body, fonts, theme, toaster, viewport). Standalone pages `/login` and `/link-bank` consequently inherit only the slim root — no tab bar, no legacy chrome.

Purpose: This is the structural foundation that lets Plan 04 add the BB chrome (`(bb)/layout.tsx` with TabBar + PageShell) without imposing it on legacy or standalone pages. Doing the legacy relocation first (Plan 03) and the BB wiring second (Plan 04) keeps each plan focused on one trust boundary and lets us verify NAV-04 (legacy preservation) and NAV-06 (standalone pages) before introducing the BB-specific layout.

Output:
- Slimmed `src/app/layout.tsx` (chrome removed, `viewportFit: "cover"` added per RESEARCH Pitfall 1)
- New `src/app/(legacy)/layout.tsx` containing the legacy chrome stack verbatim
- 5 `git mv` operations relocating `legacy/`, `analytics/`, `achievements/`, `dashboard/`, `legacy-index/` into `(legacy)/`
- One human-verify checkpoint after the moves to smoke-test all 5 legacy URLs + `/login` + `/link-bank` in the browser

This plan covers requirements **NAV-04** (legacy routes accessible without tab bar) and **NAV-06** (standalone pages render without tab bar). The verification of NAV-06 is partial here (we confirm `/login` and `/link-bank` render without legacy chrome — Plan 04's TabBar addition will complete the NAV-06 picture by confirming the tab bar is also absent).
</objective>
